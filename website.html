What's Geomatics?

Geomatics is about making cool maps and studying Earth with computers and satellites. We learn stuff like:

  • Using GPS to make accurate maps
  • Creating 3D models of places
  • Helping decide where to build things

It's like being a high-tech explorer of Earth!

Interesting Geomatics Fact

Geomatics technology was used to locate the wreck of the Titanic using advanced sonar mapping techniques!
